Alert Now Emergency System

User’s Manual

The Alert Now system is a web-based and android application developed to support the reporting, distribution, and management of emergency alerts. It is intended for use by residents, barangay officials, and relevant government agencies of San Pablo City. Residents can submit reports of emergencies through the application, while barangay officials receive and assess these reports. The system enables barangay officials to redirect reports to appropriate government agencies when necessary.

Purpose and Scope

The system enables residents to submit reports of emergencies, including road accidents, fire incidents, health emergencies, and crime-related events. Barangay officials receive, assess, and respond to these reports. Government agencies, such as the City Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (CDRRMO), Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP), and Philippine National Police (PNP), are notified when necessary. The system supports real-time communication and maintains records of incidents and responses.

User Role Access

The system defines three user roles with specific functions. Residents submit emergency reports through the android or web interface, barangay officials receive, evaluate, and manage reports for their assigned barangay, and government agency representatives from CDRRMO, BFP, and PNP receive redirected alerts and coordinate responses. Each role has a dedicated interface with functions limited to its responsibilities.

Resident

Individuals who submit emergency reports through the android or web interface.

Barangay Official

Personnel assigned to a specific barangay who receive, evaluate, and manage incoming reports.

Government Agency

Representatives from CDRRMO, BFP, and PNP who receive redirected alerts and provide appropriate response.

Each role has a dedicated interface with functions limited to its responsibilities.

User Guide

Step 1: Installing the app

To install the android application, the user performs the following steps:

  1. Visit https://now-alert-o6l9.onrender.com using an android device.
  2. Open the QR scanner and scan the QR code displayed on the Sign Up Type section.
  3. Download and install the app by following the instructions on the device.

Step 2: Resident Registration on Android App

To register as a resident on the android application, the user performs the following steps:

  1. Open the installed application.
  2. On the starter page, click the Sign Up button to access the Sign Up Type page.
  3. On the Sign Up Type page, select Resident/Barangay Official to proceed to the Sign Up page.
  4. On the Sign Up page, fill in the required details and select Resident from the role dropdown.
  5. After completing the details, click Sign Up to finish registration and proceed to the Log In page.
  6. The account is successfully created.

Step 3: Resident Log In on Android App

To log in as a resident on the android application, the user performs the following steps:

  1. Open the application.
  2. On the starter page, click Log In to access the Log In Type page.
  3. On the Log In Type page, select Resident/Official to proceed to the Log In page.
  4. On the Log In page, enter the username and password created during registration.
  5. After entering the credentials, click Log In to access the resident page.
  6. The user has successfully logged in to the resident page.

Step 4: Resident Submitting an Emergency Report

To submit an emergency report, the resident performs the following steps:

  1. Open the android application and log in following the steps in step 3.
  2. On the resident page, click the upload image option.
  3. Capture an image and click OK.
  4. Click the Alert Now button.
  5. The alert is successfully sent.

Receiving and Distributing the Alert

1. Barangay Official Receiving and Managing Reports

1. View incoming reports in the live alerts section.

2. For each report, select one of the following actions:

  • Accept the report for further handling
  • Decline the report if it does not require action
  • Redirect the report to an appropriate government agency

3. If the report is accepted, complete the response form by selecting relevant details from predefined options and submit the response.

2. Agency Notification and Response

When a barangay official redirects a report, the system automatically notifies the designated agency. Agency personnel follow these procedures:

  1. Access both the web application and android application.
  2. Receive the redirected alert through the dashboard or android notification.
  3. Review the report details, including location, description, and any attached photograph.
  4. Acknowledge receipt and, when applicable, dispatch resources or provide further instructions.
  5. The system maintains a record of all notifications and responses for reference.
